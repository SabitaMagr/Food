Indian Food

Authentic Indian recipes are full of organic ingredients that are proven to be healthy. Examples include common Indian spices like Turmeric that help with inflammation, prevention of Alzheimer’s, and improve heart health. Being plant-based, Indian cuisine is rich in a variety of grains, nuts, pulses,andseeds (that provide healthy oils). It’s the myriad health benefits of Indian cuisine that make Indian food and spices preferred on menus at many a detox retreat.
