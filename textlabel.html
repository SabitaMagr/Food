Youtube

YouTube is an American online video sharing and social media platform owned by Google . It was launched on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim .
It is the second most visited website, right after Google itself. YouTube has more than one billion monthly users[7] who collectively watch more than one billion hours of videos each day .[8] As of May 2019, videos were being uploaded at a rate of more than 500 hours of content per minute.
